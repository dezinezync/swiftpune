Code of conduct.

SwiftPune is a place to learn, teach, and belong — students and seasoned engineers in the same room. We're serious about the craft and gentle with each other. The full text of our code of conduct is being written and will live on this page shortly.

// coming soon

The complete code of conduct is on its way. In the meantime, the short version: be kind, be useful, be welcoming, and look out for the newest person in the room. If something at an event or in the community doesn't sit right, tell us — we'll take it seriously and handle it discreetly.
