About

We're SwiftPune.

A Pune-based community for anyone curious about — or already deep into — Apple's platforms. We meet to share talks, swap patterns, and help each other ship better software.

  • Learn together

    Talks, workshops, and Q&A from people actively shipping on Apple platforms.

  • Ship better

    Patterns, reviews, and feedback from a room that actually writes Swift every week.

  • Belong here

    From your first print("Hello, world!") to your tenth WWDC — there's a seat at the table.
